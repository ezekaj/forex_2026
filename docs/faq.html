Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the Ultimate Trading Bot

General

The Ultimate Trading Bot is an AI-powered multi-asset crypto trading platform. It provides real-time market data, automated trading strategies, backtesting, paper trading, and portfolio management tools. It is built by ZeDigital and deployed on Fly.io for global low-latency access.
The platform supports major cryptocurrencies: Bitcoin (BTC), Ethereum (ETH), Solana (SOL), BNB, Cardano (ADA), Polkadot (DOT), Avalanche (AVAX), and Polygon (MATIC). The Professional plan supports additional assets. All price data comes from CoinGecko.
The free Paper Trading tier uses simulated execution with real market data -- no real money is at risk. The Trader (€39/mo) and Professional (€99/mo) plans support live trading with real capital through MetaTrader 5 integration. You control when and how much real capital to deploy.
Paper trading gives you $10,000 in virtual capital. You can buy and sell crypto assets using real market prices. Your positions, P&L, trade history, and risk metrics are all tracked exactly as they would be with real money, but no actual capital is at risk. It is the best way to learn and test strategies.
The platform includes 4 built-in strategies: MA Crossover (20/50 period moving average), RSI Reversal (30/70 thresholds), MACD Signal (momentum-based), and Bollinger Bands (volatility-based). Free users get 2 strategies, Trader gets 5, and Professional gets unlimited with a custom strategy builder.
All market data is sourced from the CoinGecko API, one of the most comprehensive and reliable crypto data providers. Prices are updated in real-time via WebSocket connections. Historical data for backtesting is also from CoinGecko.

Technical

API access is available on the Professional plan. Rate limits are: REST API at 100 requests/minute, WebSocket connections limited to 5 concurrent streams. These limits are sufficient for most trading strategies and can be increased on request for enterprise users.
Professional plan users have access to the custom strategy builder. You can define entry/exit conditions using technical indicators, set custom parameters (like MA periods or RSI thresholds), and combine multiple indicators. Paper Trading and Trader plans use the 4 built-in strategies with their default parameters.
The backtester uses historical OHLCV (Open, High, Low, Close, Volume) data from CoinGecko. Free plans can backtest 7 days, Trader plans up to 90 days, and Professional plans up to 365 days of historical data.
The Trader and Professional plans connect to your MT5 broker account for live trade execution. You provide your MT5 credentials during setup, and the bot sends orders directly to your broker. Smart order routing minimizes slippage and ensures best-price fills.
The web platform is fully responsive and works well on mobile browsers. A dedicated mobile app is on the roadmap. Email and SMS alerts ensure you stay informed about trading signals even when not actively monitoring the platform.
Yes. All trade history, positions, and performance data can be exported to CSV. Professional plan users also have API access for programmatic data retrieval. This is useful for tax reporting and performance analysis.
The platform is deployed on Fly.io with automatic failover and 99.9% uptime SLA. If the primary server becomes unavailable, traffic is automatically routed to a backup instance. Open positions have stop-loss orders at the broker level, so they are protected even during platform downtime.
